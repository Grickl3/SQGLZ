The Mission of SQGLZ

SQGLZ works with small firms which provide direct value to society. SQGLZ provides these firms with diagnostic services, digging deep into fears about new ventures and pain points in existing systems. We develop smooth channels of communication and increase brand consistency. We look for broken incentive systems, counterintuitive functionality, and inelegance in designs and operations. We automate tasks when appropriate. We do our work in collaboration with our clients, not for them, and not apart from them. SQGLZ diagnoses problems. SQGLZ develops strategies. SQGLZ designs solutions.

Services

Problem Solving ❧ Web Development
Graphic Design ☙ Marketing Collateral

Shifting - recently - to focus on web development over print design and production, sqglz hopes to provide increased value to its clients through specialized service which is informed by widely varied experience.

While focusing on web development, SQGLZ maintains its position that design is and must be about solving problems. Our goal when approaching a client's project is not to build them a pretty website, but to solve the problems that the business experiences.

In 2004, a friend who was moving across the country dropped a hobby level screen printing press in my lap and said, "You went to art school; you can do this."

I started printing shirts (badly) for friend's bands and a few small businesses. Eventually, with a lot of help, I figured out what I was doing, and started branching out. After a few adventurous collaborations I founded SQGLZ in 2010. For years band shirts, handbills, posters, and album production were the mainstay. With some proding from clients and partners I decided to try my hand at making things pretty with ones and zeros. Now, I mostly write code and relish the jobs that allow me to do a little lettering or illustration.

Helpful Reading & Tools

Below, you will find a list of quality tools for getting creative and entrepreneurial tasks done. These are tools I've returned to again and again, even the ones made by us.

  • Verbing Nouns Since When is a SQGLZ project for people who need filler text, especially for the web. Programming by David Scarpetti. English syntax templates by yours truly, Deacon Rodda.
  • The Accordiomatic is actually a tool that David made for me when I was designing CSPT-79, before he came on-board the good ship SQGLZ. It creates excellent toggling divs. If you're one of many entrepreneurial types who write enough markup to Wordpress your way through representing yourself, but don't write any Javascript, then you may end up loving this tool.
  • Palleton is a fantastic color scheme tool. If you are branding yourself or just painting your bedroom this tool is most excellent.
  • Butterick's Practical Typography, read it. If your life requires writing and other people are required to read what you write then you as well as your victims will be greatly served by your having done so — that is, after you've read the eternally requisite Strunk and White.
  • Real Favicon Generator is the tool SQGLZ uses to generate the assets which show up not only as browser bar favicons but also when a site is pinned to a desktop or bookmarked. It is a simple and fantastic tool.

I hope you enjoy these as much as I have.
