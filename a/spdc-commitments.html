What commitments would we like to hold each other to?

Most social interaction is governed by implicit commitments to which the parties were never invited to opt out or amend, not openly, not with lucid deliberation. Throughout life we learn the social contract and we learn our place.

Intentional community is a privilege. Intentionality is a responsibiity.

Deacon is a sleepy Deacon.
